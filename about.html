About Campus Connect

Campus Connect is designed to streamline the management of college events, activities, and communication. It allows students, faculty, and administrators to interact efficiently.

Objectives

  • List and organize campus events.
  • Provide interactive galleries and media from events.
  • Allow students to give feedback and contact organizers.
